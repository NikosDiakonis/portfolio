Junior Front-End Developer | Aspiring Full Stack | Lifelong Learner

My journey in web development began with a 6-month eLearning course at National and Kapodistrian University of Athens. During this time, I enhanced my skills in HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript, and explored the CSS framework Bootstrap. The course also paved the way to a basic understanding of Python. Moreover, I was introduced to crucial web technologies like AJAX, jQuery, and the CMS Joomla. Alongside these studies, I took the initiative to learn Git and GitHub independently while working on various projects.

Currently, I am channeling my energy into building projects to solidify and broaden my expertise. These personal projects are not just a testament to my dedication but also a playground for experimenting with new ideas and technologies.

Parallel to this hands-on approach, I have charted a roadmap for my professional growth. This plan includes delving into WordPress and WooCommerce as also expanding my backend knowledge, focusing on learning SQL, PostgreSQL and React.js.
